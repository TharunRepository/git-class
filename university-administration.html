University Administration

Governance structure and academic administration at DSU.

The administration at DSU works through statutory bodies, academic councils and professional support teams to ensure smooth functioning of academic and research activities.

Transparent processes, well-defined policies and continuous monitoring help align day-to-day operations with the long-term vision and mission of the University.
