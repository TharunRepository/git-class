ABOUT DAYANANDA SAGAR UNIVERSITY

WELCOME TO DAYANANDA SAGAR UNIVERSITY

Dayananda Sagar Institutions founded in the 60s by late Sri Dayananda Sagar committed to take knowledge to the people, transforms today's students into responsible citizens and professional leaders of tomorrow. Dayananda Sagar University was created by an Act of the Karnataka State in 2014, built on this adorable legacy and inspired by its own milestones.

UNVEILING A LEGACY

Universities of great legacy across the world are the invaluable contribution of certain visionaries to society. They create knowledge, nurture human values and disseminate ideas that shape generations. DSU continues this tradition by bringing global quality education closer to aspiring learners.

PROUD TO BE PART OF DSI

DSU is a proud member of the Dayananda Sagar Institutions family. From a modest beginning in the early sixties, DSI has grown into a global education powerhouse, spanning multiple campuses and thousands of students. DSU carries forward this spirit of excellence, innovation and social responsibility.

BEST-IN-CLASS FACILITIES

DSU offers an academic environment with well-equipped lecture halls, modern laboratories, libraries and digital learning resources. Sports, cultural and innovation spaces on campus encourage students to explore their interests beyond the classroom.

INSPIRED BY INNOVATORS

With a strong focus on research, incubation and entrepreneurship, DSU enables students to transform ideas into real-world impact. Collaboration with industry partners, research centres and startups ensures that learning stays relevant and future ready.
