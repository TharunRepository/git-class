Admission and Eligibility for Ph.D Degree Program

Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) offers Ph.D programs in various disciplines. The University has well qualified faculty members to supervise research scholars and lead them to their doctoral degree. These programs are available in both full time and part time modes. DSU believes in the importance of interdisciplinary research and hence encourages scholars to pursue work in socially relevant areas.

Applications are invited for registration to the following Full Time / Part Time research programmes:

  1. Ph.D in the Faculty of Engineering
    • CSE Cluster (CSE-AI&ML, Data Science, Cybersecurity, AI & Robotics)
    • Electronics & Communication Engineering
    • Mechanical Engineering
    • Aerospace Engineering
    • Mathematics, Chemistry & Physics
  2. Ph.D in the Faculty of Commerce & Management Studies
  3. Ph.D in the Faculty of Basic & Applied Sciences (Life Sciences)
  4. Ph.D in the Faculty of Health Sciences
    • College of Pharmaceutical Sciences
      1. Pharmaceutics
      2. Pharmacology
      3. Pharmaceutical Chemistry
      4. Pharmacognosy
      5. Pharmaceutical Analysis
      6. Pharmacy Practice
      7. Pharmaceutical Regulatory Affairs
      8. Pharmaceutical Quality Assurance
    • College of Physiotherapy
      • Ph.D in Physiotherapy
    • College of Nursing Sciences
      • Ph.D in Nursing Sciences
  5. Ph.D in Journalism and Mass Communication (CJMC, English Literature)
  6. Ph.D in School of Law
  7. Ph.D in Computer Applications
  8. Ph.D in Para Clinical Research (Pathology & Community Medicine)
