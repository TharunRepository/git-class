PRO CHANCELLOR

Dr. D. PREMACHANDRA SAGAR

Pro Chancellor, DSU

Success comes to those who are hard working and creative. But even the best and most focused young minds need institutional leadership, a responsive culture and the right support system to truly discover their potential. At Dayananda Sagar University (DSU), we bring these together on a single platform.

As you move around the campus, you will experience an environment that blends academic rigor with opportunities for innovation, entrepreneurship, sports and cultural expression. DSU is setting its base on getting the best: the most competent individuals who are accomplished scientists, researchers and professionals from across the finest universities in the world.

National entrance tests, rigorous evaluation processes and a commitment to merit ensure that only the most accomplished students find a place in our academic programs. This approach enables us to maintain a vibrant peer group and a learning ecosystem that constantly challenges and inspires.

DSU now goes beyond traditional teaching and learning to focus on incubation, employability and entrepreneurship. Students are encouraged to walk in with their ideas and walk out with products, startups and robust career pathways. I invite you to make the best use of these opportunities and be part of DSU's exciting journey.
