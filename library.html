Central Library

Learning resources and digital services at Dayananda Sagar University.

About the Library

The Central Library at DSU supports teaching, learning and research across all schools of the University. The library houses print and digital collections, access to online journals and databases, study spaces and technology-enabled learning zones.

With extended opening hours, quiet zones and group study rooms, the library offers an ideal environment for students and researchers to explore ideas, collaborate and prepare for their academic goals.

Facilities & Services

  • Extensive collection of textbooks, reference books and research monographs
  • Online access to e-journals, e-books and academic databases
  • Digital library terminals and Wi-Fi enabled study areas
  • Circulation, reference and research support services
  • Reprography, printing and scanning facilities
