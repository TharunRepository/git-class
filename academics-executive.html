About Us:

Dayananda Sagar University, a part of the larger Dayananda Sagar Group of Institutions, was set up with an ethos of providing quality education to every learner who aspires to pursue higher education while continuing their professional journey.

Executive Education at DSU is an important vertical for the University and caters to working professionals by offering a wide bouquet of programs that allow them to acquire new skills and improve their managerial competencies.

The program design emphasizes flexible schedules, industry-led sessions, project-based learning and networking opportunities that help participants translate classroom learning into workplace impact.
