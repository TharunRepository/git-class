Late Shri R Dayananda Sagar

Founding Father

Education: Founded the Mahatma Gandhi Vidya Peetha Education Trust in the 1960s, which has since grown into a group of institutions offering programs from primary education to doctoral studies. Social Work: Was a leader in the slum development movement in Karnataka and was actively involved with labor unions as a legal advisor.
