We are in a rapidly changing world where knowledge life cycles have reduced drastically and product and process life cycles are getting even shorter. This calls for education that delivers knowledge, skill and integrated experiential learning to help learners cope with a dynamic global environment.

Dayananda Sagar University has set a vision to enable learners to excel in education, research, innovation and entrepreneurship for a sustainable tomorrow. With a multi-disciplinary campus, supportive ambience and strong industry connect, DSU prepares students to become confident professionals and responsible citizens.

Our faculty, research mentors and partners from leading institutions work closely with students to nurture their curiosity and creativity. We encourage students to pursue projects, internships and entrepreneurial ideas that contribute to society and the economy.

Welcome to Dayananda Sagar University. We wish you a bright future as you embark on your learning journey with us, and we assure you that DSU will remain a steadfast partner and mentor for life in this age of learners.
